Orange
Objectives & Workplan

Objective


  • Move Orange from multiple, eclectic & regional brand expressions to a globally recognized, coherent and unified brand identity without losing its uniqueness

  • Establish an umbrella brand that is highly differentiated in the data and communications market by being straight-forward, friendly & honest with its customers

  • Audio ID must embody a hopeful & refreshing view of a dynamic future that is globally resonant- the future is bright, the future is Orange

  • Audio ID experience system should be an empowering consumer platform

Work Plan


  • Immersion with the Orange brand team to identify rational and emotional benefits to identify brand issues, needs and competitive benefits

  • Global audio audit and competitive brand map

  • Translate Audio strategy and positioning into audio mood boards with themes and threads, music and sound selections for approval in Europe for the Orange team

  • Evaluate and refine audio ID system sounds along key customer experience paths

  • Develop audio operating system for all network, data and Orange products and test to assure brand fit, perception and evolution
