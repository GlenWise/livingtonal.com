The Cunningham Foundation
Objectives & Work Plan

Objective


  • Expand the donor list and attract the next generation of patrons and ensure the endowment is funded and solid for decades to come

  • Use the 50th anniversary of MCDC as a touchpoint for new programs, performances, collaborations and events

  • Step out of the MCDC ‘comfort zone’ and collaborate with artists that are on the cutting edge of contemporary music

  • Increase donor base and create new avenues to reach out to a younger audience.

Work Plan


  • Music should be 20 minutes in length, no formed percussion and no noticeable vocals; no formed words or lyrics

  • Can be anything the artists want to do - there will be no merging of the music and choreography until the dress rehearsal/the night before the premiere

  • Has to be performed live at dress rehearsal and for the premiere

  • Music has to be recorded for posterity with enough flexibility and variation that no two performances will be the same
  • We were able to get Sigur Ros and Radiohead to create 20 minutes of original music for Merce’s 50th anniversary.

  • In order to attract a younger and more diverse audience, we got both groups to perform their original composition live at the world premiere.

  • Convince the managers and record labels to pay for the performance out their own pocket.

  • Arranged a private meeting with Thom Yorke and Robert Rauschenberg as a condition to perform.

  • Increased new contacts and donors by 30%.
