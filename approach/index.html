We Get It
We're here for you.


Technology’s wonders are endless.
So too are the flood of devices, data & content, that drive us to distraction.

Cutting through this storm isn’t easy.
LivingTonal’s 5 step process insures that your voice will be heard, understood, remembered and recalled.

After decades of award-winning global experience, only Living Tonal can confidently deliver superior brand impact with a resonant, authentic voice that connects across countries, cultures, classes, channels, ages & genders.

We Explore, Envision, Express, Extend & Evolve.

Dive deeper into our steps

Step 1: Explore

We immerse ourselves in your brand, your competitors and the shifting culture and the category you play in. We observe, interview and share what we’ve learned.

Step 2: Envision

We imagine, strategize and map the values of your brand’s voice, business objectives and the opportunities for it to be heard, understood and remembered.

Step 3: Express

We translate your brand objectives into creative goals, developing audio mood boards, creative themes, threads and preferred sound palettes. This is the moment when your brand’s unique voice is first heard.

Step 4: Extend

The research and strategic framework we’ve designed together becomes the roadmap for all we do. It shapes every aspect of our work- as we create, curate, collect and collaborate. It’s here that your unique voice is fully realized.

Step 5: Evolve

We then help you implement, educate/train and advise on the creative roll-out. Later, we can review, revise and refresh the creative work to make sure it is a beacon and a magnet for your brand, that is both timely and timeless.
